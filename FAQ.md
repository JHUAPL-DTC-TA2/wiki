**Q: What is Workspaces and what is its role in the competition?**

A: Due to the sensitive nature of the data used for the competition, we needed to strictly monitor where the data is moving and control who has access to the data. AWS Workspaces is a Desktop-As-A-Service platform that "streams" a virtualized desktop and allows participants to interact with AWS computing resources on your local machine without the need to transfer data out of the ecosystem. Within your workspace, you should be able to access the training data, perform your analysis, and develop models.
